# Research Standards

> Documentation and quality standards applied throughout the **AI SEO Expert Research** repository.

---

# Purpose

This document defines the research methodology, documentation standards, and quality requirements used across every research document in this repository.

The objective is to ensure that all research remains consistent, accurate, traceable, and easy to maintain as the repository grows.

---

# Scope

These standards apply to all research materials contained within this repository, including:

- LinkedIn research documents
- YouTube transcript research
- Supporting research materials
- Future research additions
- Repository documentation

---

# Research Workflow

Every expert follows the same standardized workflow:

1. Expert identification
2. Identity verification
3. Official LinkedIn profile review
4. LinkedIn content analysis
5. YouTube video selection
6. Transcript review
7. Executive summary
8. Key insights extraction
9. AI SEO relevance analysis
10. Metadata collection
11. Quality verification
12. Final documentation review

---

# Source Priority

Research sources are prioritized using the following hierarchy:

| Priority | Source |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | Official LinkedIn Profile |
| 2 | Official YouTube Channel |
| 3 | Official Website |
| 4 | Conference Presentations |
| 5 | Podcast Interviews |
| 6 | Public Documentation |

Whenever possible, official sources should always be used before relying on third-party references.

---

# Research Quality Levels

| Grade | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Grade A | Official content published directly by the expert. |
| Grade B | Trusted interviews, conferences, podcasts, or guest appearances. |
| Grade C | Supporting third-party references used only for additional context. |

---

# Documentation Template

Every research document should follow a consistent structure.

Recommended sections include:

- Identity / Video Information
- Executive Summary
- Research Summary
- Discussion Timeline (Video Research)
- Key Insights
- Actionable Takeaways
- AI SEO Relevance
- Mentioned Concepts
- Mentioned Resources
- Research Metadata
- Verification Checklist
- Research Status

---

# Metadata Requirements

Each research document should include:

- Expert Name
- Source URL
- Publication Date
- Platform
- Research Topic
- Research Category
- Document Version
- Last Updated

---

# Verification Checklist

Before marking a research document as complete, verify that:

- [ ] Expert identity has been verified
- [ ] Official profile has been confirmed
- [ ] Official source has been used whenever possible
- [ ] Metadata is complete
- [ ] Summary is written in original words
- [ ] Formatting follows repository standards
- [ ] Internal links are correct
- [ ] External links are valid
- [ ] Research status has been updated
