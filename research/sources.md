# Selection Criteria

Experts were selected based on the following criteria:

- Demonstrated expertise in SEO and AI-powered content production.
- Active publication of educational content on LinkedIn and/or YouTube.
- Practical industry experience supported by case studies or real-world implementation.
- Frequent contributor to discussions on Google Search, AI Search, content strategy, or technical SEO.
- Strong reputation and recognition within the SEO community.
- Availability of publicly accessible educational resources suitable for research.

# Research Methodology

The research process followed a standardized workflow for every expert:

1. Verify expert identity and official profiles.
2. Review LinkedIn profile and recent professional activity.
3. Collect and summarize recent LinkedIn posts.
4. Identify and review relevant YouTube content.
5. Record metadata, publication dates, and source references.
6. Produce structured research documents using a consistent documentation template.
7. Cross-check findings against official websites or supporting materials when available.
