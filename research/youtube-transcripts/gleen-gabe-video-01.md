In this analysis, Glenn Gabe examines the impact of Google's May 2026 Core Update, one of the most significant algorithm updates of the year. Drawing from extensive observations across multiple industries, countries, and websites, Glenn explains how the update affected rankings, AI Search manipulation, YMYL websites, and Google's broader quality evaluation systems.

# Transcript Summary

Glenn begins by explaining the rollout timeline of Google's May 2026 Core Update and compares its impact with the March 2026 update, describing it as substantially more volatile.

He then analyzes industries that experienced significant ranking fluctuations, particularly YMYL websites and gambling-related content. The presentation also investigates whether websites attempting to manipulate AI Search visibility experienced ranking losses during the update.

Several case studies are presented, including websites recovering from earlier algorithm updates, manual action interactions, Grokipedia's unexpected growth, and Google's handling of large-scale translated content.
